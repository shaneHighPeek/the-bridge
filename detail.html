About Dental Health Care Associates

Dental Health Care Associates (DHCA) Dentists Melbourne CBD at Level 4/175 Collins Street VIC 3000. General dentistry, regular check-ups and dental hygiene appointments are an important part of your complete oral health program.

DHCA offers a full range of cosmetic treatments such as teeth whitening, crowns and bridges and porcelain veneers. Your oral health program may also require special treatments like dental implants or CEREC restorations. DHCA also specialise in Children's oral health treatments.

Ask about the DHCA 5 Steps program. It's been designed and developed for each and every DHCA patient. Part of our 'Preventative Approach' to your dental health and hygiene.
