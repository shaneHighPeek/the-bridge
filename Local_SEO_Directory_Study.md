# Feasibility Study: Local AI-SEO Directory
**Status:** Shane-Ready Review
**Target:** Niche Services Australia

## 1. Concept: The "Invisible Infrastructure"
This project is a programmatic SEO engine. It automates the creation of high-ranking local service directories without manual content writing.

## 2. Target Niches (High Intent / Fragmented Competition)
- **NDIS Providers (SE QLD):** High lead value, complex search terms.
- **Mobile EV Mechanics:** Emerging market, zero established local SEO dominance.
- **Boutique Aged Care:** High trust requirement, localized search.
- **Specialist Tradies:** Underpinning, structural, niche landscaping.

## 3. The Automation Pipeline
1. **Ingest:** Scrape business details from Google Maps & industry registries.
2. **Enrich:** AI generates unique, local-context landing pages for each business.
3. **Deploy:** Programmatic deployment to a high-speed Next.js frontend.
4. **Index:** Automated submission to Google Search Console for instant crawling.

## 4. Monetization Strategy
- **Stage 1 (Architect):** Subscription model ($99/mo) for the "Top 3" featured slots in each suburb.
- **Stage 2 (Builder):** Pay-Per-Lead (PPL). Once traffic hits, we sell the enquiries directly to the businesses.

## 5. Pros & Cons
- **Pros:** Fast to scale; creates a "moat" of indexed pages; high ROI once ranked.
- **Cons:** Google algorithm sensitivity; requires high-quality "Helpful Content" to stay indexed.

## 6. Phase 1 Roadmap
- **Pilot:** "NDIS Gold Coast" directory launch.
- **Goal:** Validate indexing speed and lead capture functionality.
